Create a Zone

In the past any action could be carried out, at any time, in your name. If an impostor obtained enough information about you to satisfy an organisations criteria they could carry out an action as you. When you use Zonafide no action can be carried out, at anytime, without you declaring that a Zone exists for the action.

This is so different to how organisations have you behave today that you need to experience it. Let's create our first Zone.

Protect an Action with a Zone

To start to protect an action your first create a zone and invite others to acknowledge you intend to perform an action. You do not specify what the action is yet. At all times Zonafide ensures only just enough information is used to make your action possible. This stops information leakage. You might be used to organisations asking you for as much information as possible. That is because the information is of interest to them but not necessarily in your interests to reveal it.

Zone Members

Provide the address of someone who you would like to acknowledge the action protected by the zone. This makes it much more difficult for an imposter to carry out an action as you because they would have to compromise that person to. The more members that you have to acknowledge your zone the harder it would be to compromise you.
